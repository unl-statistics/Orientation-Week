Teaching and Orientation (UNL-wide)
  • UNL Grad Studies Orientation (Canvas)
  • UNL Grad Studies Institute for International Teaching Assistants
    This program takes place 2 weeks before orientation week and is extremely valuable for both domestic and international students. It is required for international students (unless you have a Masters degree from an English-language university).
Information for Incoming Students

Our department’s Graduate Student Association (GSA) maintains an informal handbook for new graduate students that includes information such as

  • how to register for classes
  • how to get UNL ID card and bus pas
  • how to connect to campus WiFi
  • housing resources
  • places to go in Lincoln

and much, much more. Check it out!
